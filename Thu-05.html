Programm for Thursday 04-June-2026

14:00 Door opens
Samgar, Thorsten and the check-in volunteers will be at the venue and wait for everyone to arrive.
The idea for Thursday is to do very little, but to be there as people arrive. Usually this works out well as the participants can sit, socialize, and have a good time.
Corpus Christi is a public holiday, so while many restaurants etc. will be open, shops will be generally closed.
To make sure that nobody is starving, we provide drinks and food.
22:00 Door closes
We'll call it a day at 22:00h. The old Blues Brothers rule applies: “You don't have to go home but you can't stay here.”
