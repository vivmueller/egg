2. Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which publishes the static files stored in the repository. The website itself does not use a separate backend server or database operated by us.

3. Data processed when you use this website

We do not actively collect personal data through the website itself. There is no contact form, no user account, no upload form, and no message function.

  • No personal data is entered into this website by default.
  • No analytics, tracking, advertising, or profiling tools are used.
  • No cookies are used for tracking purposes.
  • We do not store visitor data on our own server.

4. Ticket function

The app contains a ticket function. If you save a ticket in the app, the chosen file is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. The file remains on your device and is not uploaded to us.

You can remove the saved ticket at any time in the app or by clearing the website data in your browser.

5. Schedule data and external services

Event schedule content is loaded from a Google Apps Script JSON endpoint. When your browser requests this content, it contacts Google directly in order to load the data.

The website also contains external links to third-party websites such as maps, transport services, and information pages. When you open those links, the privacy policies and terms of the respective third-party providers apply.

6. Contact by e-mail

If you contact us by e-mail, we will use the information you send only to handle your request and reply to you.

7. Storage period

Since we do not run a form, account system, or server-side user database, we do not store visitor data. Data saved locally in your browser stays there until you delete it yourself.

8. Your rights under the GDPR

You have the right to request information about any personal data stored about you, as well as the right to rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, objection, and data portability, subject to the legal requirements. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority.

9. Security note

Internet data transmission can never be completely secure. When you open an external link, the data is handled by the relevant provider or service.
